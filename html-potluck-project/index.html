Bean Burritos

Ingredients

  • 1 can (16 ounces) vegetarian refried beans
  • 1 cup of salsa
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 12 flour tortillas (6 inches)
  • shredded lettuce, optional

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In a large bowl, combine beans, salsa, rice and 1 cup of cheese
  2. Spoon about 1/3 cup mixture off-cente on each tortilla
  3. Fold the sides and ends over filing and roll up.
