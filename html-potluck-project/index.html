Bean Burritos

Ingredients
  • 1 can (16ounces) vegetarian refriend beans
  • 1 cup cooked salsa
  • 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese optional
  • 12 flour tottillas (6 inches)
  • Shredded lettuce, optional

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In a large bowl, combine beans, salsa, rice, and 1 cup of cheese
  2. Spoon about 1/3 cup mixture off-center on each tortilla
  3. Fold the sides and ends over filing and roll up
  4. Arrange burrito in a greased 13x9 inch baking until heated through, 20-25minutes. if desired top with lettece

Salsa Steak Garlic Toast

Ingredients
  • 4 slices frozen garlic texas toast
  • 1 table spoon oive oil
  • 1 beef top sirloin steak, thin sliced
  • 1-1/2 cups of salsa
  • Sour Cream and chopped fresh cilantro
Directions
  1. Prepare garlic toats
  2. Meanwhile, in a large skillet, heat oil over medium meat
